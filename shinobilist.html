Hokages

  1. Hashirama Senju

One of the god level shinobis who created the Leaf Village and stopped war between nations. Created Academy for new generations to learn new jutsus.

  1. Tobirama Senju

Brother of Hashirama, also known for being one of the fastest shinobis. Invented the Flying Raijin jutsu and Created ANBU and Leaf Police Force.

  1. Hiruzen Sarutobi

Known as the Professor due to his mastery in all five chakra natures and a number of jutsus. His ability to learn a new Jutsu in no time is exceptional.

  1. Minato Namikaze

The Yellow Flash of the village, feared nationwide due to his superfast speed and unmatchable skills. Rediscovered Rasengan and saved the village.

  1. Tsunade Senju

One of the only 3 Legendary Sanins, known as the Slug Queen and the best Medical Ninja nationwide. Kept Hashirama's Will of Fire burning.

  1. Kakashi Hatake

He's been a skilled and exceptional Shinobi ever since he was a kid. Joined ANBU at the age of 12, invented the Lightning Blade and became Sixth Hokage.

  1. Naruto Uzumaki

One of the strongest shinobis presently, he's the main reason why shinobi world has achieved peace. Jinchuriki to the nine-tails and has chakra of all tailed beasts.

Akatsuki

  1. Yahiko

Yahiko was a shinobi from Hidden Rain Village. Alongside his fellow war orphans, Nagato and Konan, he founded and led the Akatsuki in an attempt to bring peace.

  1. Nagato (Pain)

Nagato was a shinobi of Rain Village and descendant of the Uzumaki clan. Forming Akatsuki alongside his friends (and fellow war orphans) Yahiko and Konan.

  1. Konan

Konan was a kunoichi from Rain Village and a founding member of the original Akatsuki. After Yahiko's death, she was partnered with Nagato.

  1. Zetsu

Black Zetsu was the physical manifestation of Princess Kaguya Ōtsutsuki's will. It secretly instigated many of the events that shaped the shinobi world.

  1. Itachi Uchiha

Itachi Uchiha was a shinobi of Hidden Leaf's Uchiha clan that served as an Anbu Captain. He later became an international criminal after murdering his clan.

  1. Kisame Hoshigaki

Kisame Hoshigaki was a shinobi of Hidden Mist's Hoshigaki Clan. After joining the Seven Ninja Swordsmen of the Mist, he became a S-rank missing-nin.

  1. Jūzō Biwa

Jūzō Biwa was a jōnin from Hidden Mist and a member of the Seven Ninja Swordsmen of the Mist. Jūzō deserted his village and became a member of Akatsuki.

  1. Orochimaru

Orochimaru is one of Hidden Leaf's legendary Sannin. With a life-ambition to learn all of the world's secrets.

  1. Obito Uchiha (Tobi)

Obito Uchiha was a member of Konohagakure's Uchiha clan. He was believed to have died during the Third Shinobi World War.

  1. Deidara

Deidara was an S-rank missing-nin from Iwagakure. He was a member of the Explosion Corps. After defecting from the village, he was forced into Akatsuki.

  1. Sasori

Sasori was a missing-nin from Sunagakure's Puppet Brigade and a member of Akatsuki, where he was partnered with Orochimaru and later Deidara.

  1. Kakuzu

Kakuzu was an S-rank missing-nin from Takigakure and a member of Akatsuki who was partnered with Hidan.

  1. Hidan

Hidan is an S-rank missing-nin who defected from Yugakure and later joined the Akatsuki. There, he was partnered with Kakuzu.
